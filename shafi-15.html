Burujin Saratan (Cancer) ♋

Burujin Saratan shi ne buruji na hudu a lissafin falaki. Wannan buruji yana da matukar muhimmanci domin shi ne uwa a cikin burujai (Mother of the Zodiac). Mutanen wannan burujin suna da matukar tausayi, rikon amana, da kuma son kyautautawa iyalansu da makwabtansu.

Dabi'arsa da Kaukabinsa

Dabi'arsa: Ruwa (Water). Kamar yadda ruwa yake mai sanyi da kwantar da hankali amma kuma yana iya yin ambaliya, haka mutanen Saratan suke; suna da sanyin hali amma kuma idan aka taba su suna da zafin kishi da fushi.
Kaukabinsa: Wata (Moon). Wata ne yake mulkin wannan buruji, shi ya sa yanayin jin dadi ko bacin ransu yakan sauya lokaci-lokaci kamar yadda wata yake girma ko yake raguwa.

Ranar Sa'arsa da Dalili

Ranar sa'ar masu burujin Saratan ita ce Ranar Litinin. Dalili a ilimin lissafin kaukabai shine, Litinin ita ce ranar da Kaukabin Wata (Moon) yake gudanar da mulkinsa gadan-gadan. Yin manyan harkoki a wannan ranar yana kara musu haske da karbuwa.

Sana'o'in da Suka Dace

  • Aikin Asibiti da Jinya (Nursing): Saboda kwarewarsu wajen kula da mutane da tausayi.
  • Sayar da Abinci da Kayan Gida: Suna da fasahar hada abinci masu dadi saboda son dangi.
  • Kasuwancin Filaye da Gidaje (Real Estate): Tunda suke son gina gida na kansu, suna da sa'a a harkar gidaje.
  • Harkar Ruwa ko Kifi: Sana'o'in da suka shafi ruwa suna kawo musu albarka mai yawa.

Sadaka da Turarensa

Sadaka: Mafi kyawun sadaka ga masu Saratan ita ce bayar da abinci, madara mai kyau, ko kuma fararen tufafi ga mabukata don samun budin kofofin alheri.
Turare: Turaruka masu sanyi irin su 'Misk', 'Rose', da 'Jasmine' suna taimakawa matuka wajen kwantar musu da hankali.

Addu'o'i da Sirrin Nasara

Babban sirrin nasarar su shine kiyaye saba wa iyaye da yawan kyauta. Addu'ar da ta fi dacewa da su ita ce yawaita ambaton "Ya Rahman, Ya Rauf, Ya Wadud" (Mai yawan jin kai, Mai matukar Tausayi, Mai yawan So/Kauna) don Allah ya kara musu farin jini da kariya.

Karin Ilimi: Shigar Rana Cikin Burujin Saratan ☀️

Rana tana shiga cikin burujin Saratan a tsakanin 21 ga Yuni zuwa 22 ga Yuli na kowace shekara. Wannan shine lokacin da ake kira (Summer Solstice) a kasashen yamma inda rana ta fi kowane lokaci tsayi, kuma yana haifar da manyan sauye-sauye a halayyar mutane.

Tasirin Shigar Rana a Saratan:

  • Bunkasar Tausayi da Kaunar Juna: A wannan kakar, mutane sukan fi son zama da iyalansu, da kyautautawa makusantansu.
  • Daidaita Tunanin Dan Adam: Wanda yake cikin damuwa yakan samu nutsuwa a wannan lokacin domin kaukabin wata yana isar da sako na salama.
  • Kula da Lafiya da Gyaran Gida: Wannan shine lokaci mafi kyau da ake samun kwarin gwiwar gyara gine-gine da kuma kula da lafiyar jiki (Dieting).
