Lissafin Falaki: Umar da Khadija

Assalamu Alaikum barkanmu da wannan lokaci. Ni ne Sulaiman Maiduguri, Malamin Zaure mai yi muku bayani akan ilimin Falaki da Hisabi.

1. Lissafin Abajada

Domin gano tauraron mutum, muna amfani da lissafin sunansa da na mahaifiyarsa kamar haka:

Sunan Umar (عمر): Jimilla = 310

Sunan Khadija (خديجة): Jimilla = 622

Jimillar Gaba Daya: 310 + 622 = 932.

Idan aka raba 932 da gida 12, saura za ta zama 8.

2. Buruji da Kaukabi

Sakamakon lissafin ya nuna cewa tauraron wannan bawan Allah shi ne:

  • BURUJIN AKRABU (Scorpio)
  • Yanayi (Element): Ruwa (Water).
  • Kaukabi (Planet): Mirrikh (Mars).

3. Ranar Sa'a

Ranar sa'ar wannan tauraro ita ce Ranar Talata. Wannan rana tana da matukar muhimmanci ga mai wannan tauraron wajen fara sabon abu, tafiya, ko kulla yarjejeniya.

4. Hulda da Auratayya

Wannan tauraro na Akrabu yana dacewa sosai da mutanen da suke da taurarin:

  • Saratanu (Cancer)
  • Hutu (Pisces)
  • Thauru (Taurus) – Wannan shi ne kishiyarsa wanda suke dacewa a aure.

5. Sana'o'i (A takaice)

Wasu daga cikin sana'o'in da suka dace da mai wannan tauraron:

  • Jami'in tsaro (Police/Military)
  • Likitan fida (Surgeon)
  • Kasuwancin kayan ruwa
  • Lauya mai kare hakki
  • Kasuwancin magunguna
  • Fawa (Kasuwancin nama)
  • Kasuwancin karafa
  • Injinan hakar ma'adinai
  • Aikin jarida na bincike
  • Kasuwancin man fetur

6. Sadaka & Zikiri

Sadaka: Nama (Musamman ja), Tufafi ja, Ruwa mai tsafta.

Zikiri: YA QAHHARU, YA JABBARU (Adadin 310 ko 1,106).

7. Dabi'u

Na Gari: Mai zurfin tunani, mai rikon amana, jarumi.

Marasa Kyau: Saurin fushi, zurfin ciki, daukar fansa.
