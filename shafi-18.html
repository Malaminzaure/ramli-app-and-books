Koma Jerin Darussa
Darasi na 18 Falaki / Burujai

Burujin Mizan (Libra) ♎

Burujin Mizan shi ne buruji na bakwai a jerin burujai. Tambarin wannan burujin shine "Ma'auni" (Scales). Wannan na nuni da cewa masu wannan burujin mutane ne da suke son adalci, daidaito, zaman lafiya, da kuma tsantsar gaskiya a kowane al'amari na rayuwarsu.

Dabi'arsa da Yanayin Halittarsa

Dabi'ar Mizan Iska ce (Air Element). Kamar yadda iska take shiga ko'ina ba tare da an ganta ba, haka suke da kwarewa wajen shiga mutane. Suna da yawan fara'a, iya magana, da son ganin kowa ya amfana. Suna kyamar zalunci ko danne hakkin wani, kuma ba sa son hayaniya ko fada. Ba za ka taba ganin asalin fushinsu ba sai an ki yi musu adalci.

Kaukabinsa da Ranar Sa'arsa

Kaukabin da ke mulkin Mizan shi ne Zuhura (Venus). Wannan kaukabin shine gungun taurarin soyayya, kyau, dukiya, da abubuwan more rayuwa. Shi yasa za ka ga masu Mizan suna son wanka, kyale-kyale, da rayuwa mai dadi.

Sirrin Ranar Sa'a (Juma'a): Ranar Juma'a ita ce ranar Zuhura, don haka ita ce ranar haske ga masu Mizan. Duk wata harkar soyayya, neman aure, kulla sabuwar alaqa ta kasuwanci, ko rokon wata babbar bukata, yin hakan a ranar Juma'a zai sa su ga nasara fiye da tunaninsu.

Sana'o'in da Suka Fi Dace Da Su

Tunda masu Mizan ba sa son wahala mai sanya karfi sosai, kuma suna son aiki cikin al'umma, wadannan sana'o'in sun fi bude musu kofar arziki:

  • Aikin Shari'a (Lawyer/Judge): Saboda kyamar zalunci da son gaskiya.
  • Dillancin Kaya / Kasuwanci (Brokerage): Suna da basirar hada masu saye da masu sayarwa ta hanyar lalabi.
  • Kwalliya da Kayan Adon Mata (Cosmetics/Boutique): Tunda kaukabinsu na kyau ne (Zuhura), sana'ar atamfa, turaruka, da kayan kwalliya yana basu kudi sosai.
  • Harkar Tattaunawa da Sulhu (Public Relations/Diplomacy): Duk aikin da ya shafi magana a madadin kamfani.

Turare, Sadaka, da Kalar Sa'a

Kalar da ta fi yi musu kyau kuma take jawowa masu Mizan sa'a ita ce Hoda (Pink) ko Koriyar Sama (Light Blue).

Mafi Kyawun Turare: Turaren Jasmine, Ward (Rose), ko Sandalwood suna bude idon basirarsu, suna kawo musu farin jini, da sanya musu natsuwa musamman lokacin da suke cikin damuwa.

Sirrin Sadaka: Sadaka da Zaki-zaki (kamar Alawa, Sugar, ko Dabino), da Turare masu dadi suna kankare musu asirin makiya tare da bude musu sabbin hanyoyin kudi.

Zikiri da Sirrin Nasararsu

Masu Mizan sukan yi gaggawar yanke hukunci akan mutane, sannan wani lokacin suna shakku wajen zartar da abin da suka tsara (indecisiveness). Addu'a ita ce mafita a gare su.

Addu'ar Zikiri: Domin samun karfin gwiwa, kwarjini a idon mutane, da samun kudi masu yawa cikin sauki, a yi wannan zikirin:

"Ya 'Adil, Ya Wudud, Ya Jami'" (Mafi Adalci, Mai yawan Kauna, Mai Hadawa)
  • Adadi: Kafa 114 ko 313.
  • Lokaci: Ana so a yi shi a daren Juma'a (wato Alhamis da daddare) ko bayan sallar Asuba ranar Juma'a.

Karin Ilimi: Tasirin Shigar Rana Cikin Burujin Mizan ☀️

A ilimin lissafin sararin samaniya, Rana tana shiga cikin gidan Mizan a tsakanin 23 ga Satumba zuwa 22 ga Oktoba na kowace shekara. Wannan lokaci ne da duniya take samun daidaito inda dare da rana suke zama tsawonsu daya a lokacin kaka (Autumn Equinox).

Me hakan ke nufi ga Halittar Dan Adam?

  • Daidaito a Tunanin Mutane: A wannan kakar, mutane sukan rage fushi, fada ya kan ragu tsakanin kasashe, sannan sulhu da yafiya sukan yi sauri.
  • Bunkasar Alaqa da Aure: Tunda burujin kyakkyawar alaqa ne, an fi samun yawan daurin aure, kulla amincewa, da fahimtar juna tsakanin masoya a wannan lokacin.
  • Habakar Kasuwancin Kawa: Duk wani dillancin tufafi, teloli, masu gyaran jiki da na gashi suna samun ciniki mai yawa a wannan lokacin fiye da sauran lokuta a shekara.
