Muhimmancin Darasin nan:

Sanin wannan matakin yanada matukar muhimmanci dole sai kasanshi kafin ka iya cire wasu hukunce-hukunce. Saboda dashi zaka iya gane mutum dan gari ne ko dan waje, da duk wani abinda ya danganci haka.saboda haka yan uwa ahaddace wannan matakin zakuga naware taurarin daban daban ga dakilai ga karijai ga sabitai ga munkalabai kuma anaso kowanne kahaddacesu fatan alkairi
