DABI'UN TAURARI (Kashi Hudu)

Kasani mai karatu!

Wannan yana daya daga cikin darussa masu muhimmanci saboda haka doki ka haddace wannan darasin. Kasani su taurarin ramli sun kasu kashi 4:

  • 🔥 NARI - Masu dabi'ar wuta
  • 💨 HAWA'I - Masu dabi'ar iska
  • 💧 MA'I - Masu dabi'ar ruwa
  • 🌍 TURABI - Masu dabi'ar kasa

Ga rabe rabensu a kasa, ya zama dole ka haddace kowanne saboda nan gaba dole sai ka nemi darasin.
