YADDA AKE FITAR DA ADADIN SUNA

Lura Mai Kyau Dalibai!

Wannan matakin shine mafi muhimmanci a ilimin Hisabi gaba daya. Idan zaka duba wa mutum burujinsa, ko zaka duba dacewar ma'aurata, ko zaka cire masa addu'a da lokaci, Dole sai ka hada sunansa da sunan mahaifiyarsa ka cire adadinsu a lissafi.

GARGADI: Tunda yanzu ka gama matakan sanin haruffa, dole sai ka san yadda ake hada su. Idan kuwa har yanzu baka haddace adadin kowane harafi ba a cikin abjadiyya, to hanzarta ka koma Mataki na 1 ka haddace kafin ka wuce nan!

HANYAR FITAR DA LISSAFI CIKIN SAUKI

Ka'idar itace: Zaka rubuta sunan mutum a haruffan Larabci daidai yadda ake fadar sa. Sannan ka dauki adadin kowane harafi guda daya ka rubuta a kasa, sai ka tattara su (addition/plus) guri daya don samun jimullar adadin.

Don ka kara ganewa tunda bamu da damar tambaya a nan, ga misalai har guda biyar (5) a aikace:

MISALI NA 1: Sunan SULAIMAN (سليمان)

Idan muka raba haruffan sunan Sulaiman a Larabci zamu samu: س ل ي م ا ن. Yanzu bari mu kawo adadin kowane harafi:

  • س (Sin) = 60
  • ل (Lam) = 30
  • ي (Ya) = 10
  • م (Mim) = 40
  • ا (Alif) = 1
  • ن (Nun) = 50
Jimulla (Total): 60 + 30 + 10 + 40 + 1 + 50 = 191

MISALI NA 2: Sunan FATIMA (فاطمة)

Idan muka raba haruffan sunan mace Fatima: ف ا ط م ة. Lura cewa karshen sunan Ta'Marbuta ne (ة) wanda a lissafi yana matsayin Ha (ه). Ga adadin:

  • ف (Fa) = 80
  • ا (Alif) = 1
  • ط (Ta) = 9
  • م (Mim) = 40
  • ة (Ta/Ha) = 5
Jimulla (Total): 80 + 1 + 9 + 40 + 5 = 135

MISALI NA 3: Sunan MUHAMMAD (محمد)

Wannan sunan mafi girma, haruffansa sune: م ح م د. Koda yake ana yi wa Mim din karshe Shadda a fada, amma a rubutu Mim biyu ce. Ga lissafin:

  • م (Mim) = 40
  • ح (Ha) = 8
  • م (Mim) = 40
  • د (Dal) = 4
Jimulla (Total): 40 + 8 + 40 + 4 = 92

MISALI NA 4: Sunan Allah (الله)

Idan za a fitar da adadin aiki don ciro addu'ar neman bukata da sunan Allah, haruffansa sune: ا ل ل ه.

  • ا (Alif) = 1
  • ل (Lam) = 30
  • ل (Lam) = 30
  • ه (Ha) = 5
Jimulla (Total): 1 + 30 + 30 + 5 = 66

MISALI NA 5: Fitar Da Suna Tare Da Mahaifiya

Idan zamu fitar wa mutum Burujinsa, lissafin yana bukatar a hada Sunan Mutum + Sunan Mahaifiyarsa.

Misali, yarinya sunanta LADIFA (لطيفة) sannan sunan mahaifiyarta HAWA'U (حواء).

Sunan Mutum: لطيفة
  • ل (30) + ط (9) + ي (10) + ف (80) + ة (5)

Jimulla = 134

Mahaifiya: حواء
  • ح (8) + و (6) + ا (1) + ء (1)

Jimulla = 16

JIMULLAR GABA DAYA: 134 + 16 = 150

Ana samun wannan jimullar (150) a hannu, da ita ce za'a raba a matakin gaba domin gano tauraron / burujin mutum! amma akwai dan bayani akan adadin sunan hauwau sai a darasi nagaba zamuyi amma asalin adadin shine 15 amma agaba zamuyi bayanin yadda yazama 15
