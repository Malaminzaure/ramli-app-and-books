HARUFFAN HASKE DA NA DUHU

Muhimmancin Wannan Darasin

A cikin ilimin Hisabi da asirai, haruffan Larabci guda 28 sun kasu kashi biyu: Haruffa 14 na Haske (Hurufun Nuraniyya) da kuma Haruffa 14 na Duhu (Hurufun Zulmaniyya).

Wannan ilimin yana da matukar muhimmanci! Idan kana aikin neman budi, farin jini, nasara, ko warkarwa, ana amfani da Haruffan Haske ne. Amma idan ana aikin rufe mugun baki, boye asiri, kore iska, ko karya sihiri, to ana amfani da Haruffan Duhu ne.

HARUFFAN HASKE (Guda 14)

Ga haruffan haske guda 14 a warwatse:

نصحكيملهسرقاطع

Sirrin Saukin Hadda (Ramzi)

نَصٌّ    حَكِيمٌ    لَهُ    سِرٌّ    قَاطِعٌ

Nassun Hakimun Lahu Sirrun Qadi'un

HARUFFAN DUHU (Guda 14)

Ga sauran haruffan guda 14 na duhu a warwatse:

بتثجخدذزشضظغفو

Sirrin Saukin Hadda (Rera su)

بَتَثَجُ    خَدَذَزُ    شَضَظَغُ    فَوَ

Ba-ta-tha-ju    Kha-da-dha-zu    Sha-da-za-ghu    Fa-wa

Gargaɗi:

A tabbatar an haddace wadannan haruffan daki-daki sannan a haddace kalmomin tasu. Domin a gaba zamu yi amfani da su wajen sanin ko aikin da mutum yake so a yi masa zai amsu ko zai yi wuya!
